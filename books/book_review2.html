Books Review

Ikigai

4.5/5

"Ikigai: The Japanese Secret to a Long and Happy Life" by Héctor García and Francesc Miralles explores the concept of "Ikigai," which can be roughly translated as the reason for being. The authors share insights from the lives of Japanese centenarians who practice this philosophy and provide guidance on how readers can discover their own ikigai.

The book blends practical advice with cultural insights, examining how a balanced life filled with purpose can lead to fulfillment and longevity. It offers readers actionable steps to find their passion, mission, and vocation, all while staying connected with their inner purpose and the world around them.

"Ikigai" encourages a simple yet meaningful approach to life that can lead to greater happiness, health, and long-term satisfaction.
